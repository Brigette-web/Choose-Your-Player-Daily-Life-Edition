Welcome to Brigette’s Daily Life

Hi! I’m Brigette Fionna E. Soria, and this is a glimpse into my everyday life. It may be boring to some, but to me, it’s just how I roll—school, sleep, random thoughts, and, of course, a little bit of fun. Whether it’s waking up at dawn, taking pictures of the sky, or getting lost in made-up scenarios before bed, my routine is simple but uniquely mine. Want to know more? Keep reading!

I wake up at 4:00 AM—but let’s be honest, I don’t get up right away. I lay in bed, staring at the ceiling, questioning my life decisions (just kidding… or maybe not). Sometimes, I create random scenarios in my head before actually starting my day. Once I feel awake enough, I begin my morning routine take a bath, wear my school uniform, eat breakfast, brush my teeth, prepare myself for school. Before heading out, I always take a moment to admire the sky—maybe even snap a quick picture.

After school, I change into my comfy clothes, eat lunch, and… lay down all day. Yes, I love sleeping, so I usually nap in the afternoon. When I wake up, I work on my homework and activities. Once that’s done, it’s finally phone time—scrolling through TikTok, chatting with friends, or reading. Before bed, I eat dinner, brush my teeth, and… back to creating scenarios in my head. Some are so tragic they make my heart ache (or even make me cry, lol). Then, I finally drift off to sleep.

On weekends, I get to sleep in until 7:00 AM—a much-needed break from my early weekday alarms. My routine is pretty simple: eat breakfast, brush my teeth, do my assigned household chores, do homework (if there is one), use my phone (of course!). Sometimes, I practice playing the keyboard, but let’s be real—there are days when I’m just too lazy to do it. Still, I’m learning at my own pace!

And that’s pretty much my daily life. It’s a mix of school, naps, scenarios in my head, and lots of phone time.
