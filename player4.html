Welcome to Rachelle’s Daily Life

Welcome to my daily life blog! I'm Rachelle Mae M. Basalan. Sharing my daily experiences, ideas, and discoveries with you makes me very happy. I use my blog as a place to record the small details that add interest to life, whether they are ordinary or extraordinary. Join me as we explore the beauty and chaos of daily life, one post at a time. Let's connect!

My alarm clock screams at 4:30 AM. I don't like it, but I get up. First, I go to the bathroom and getting ready for shower, toothbrush, wearing a school uniform, and do my skincare routine. After doing my skincare, I drink milk only because I don’t eat breakfast before I go to the school. I check my schedule, grab my bag and phone, and finally, head out the door, ready to face whatever the school day may bring.

After my school, I go home and not going anywhere because so many homework will do, and I preferred at home rather than going somewhere else. Every afternoon, I sleep 3 hours before doing my homework but before that, I read my Our Daily Bread for 1 hour and doing my devotional.

When I got bored, I always playing music, playing with my cats and playing online game. It's a chance to unwind, to express myself, and to escape the pressures of school. This is my time to recharge and reconnect with what truly matters. And before I sleep, I thank God for his goodness and guiding me through my life.

Life's a whirlwind, let's explore it together. Small moments, big discoveries. Faith, family, and fun along the way.
